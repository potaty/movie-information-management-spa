What's this?

This movie management system can help you manage the movie you watched. You can easily maintain the movie information such as name, director, year and also you can add your own commments.

Where is the data?

Save in localStorage, which is in the browser.

What operations can I do with movies in this system?

  • edit
  • delete
  • update
  • search movie by name

Do you have any 404 page?

Yes. Here it is. Or you can have a try with invalid string in url. It will redirect to 404 page.

What if I have two movies which have the same name?

The name of the movie needs to be unique. Or it would give you a warning message, until you change the name.
